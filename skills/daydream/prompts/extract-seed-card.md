# Extract Seed Card

Read the selected seed document and return strict JSON matching the Daydream seed-card template.

Extract:

1. the seed's core meaning,
2. the one claim it most wants to express or prove,
3. concepts at surface, mechanism, and meta levels that can become semantic searches,
4. tensions that deserve echoes,
5. mechanisms that may appear in other domains,
6. failure modes that may rhyme elsewhere,
7. questions worth sending back into the corpus,
8. topic-only search directions to avoid,
9. short exact evidence spans from the seed.

Write semantic `search_text` for concepts, mechanisms, and failure modes. Phrase those texts for meaning-based search, not grep. Do not invent evidence that the seed does not provide.
